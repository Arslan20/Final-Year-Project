Welcome to the John Dalton Building Panorama Virtual Tour




This is my Project called Panorama- Based University Tour. The aim of this project was to design and implement a 360 degree panorama tour of the John Dalton Building. The purpose of constructing this project together is to allow potential future students who travel from far distances who plan to visit the univeristy to explore the facilities available in the John Dalton Building.

By constructing this project, it allows students to explore around the John Dalton building via hotspots and see the different locations and facilities available. Alternative navigation is available within the John Dalton panorama tour by clicking on the Google map button in the controller. Clicking on the pin point markers allows you to navigate quickly and effectively to the known locations in the John Dalton Building.

The locations which are in this John Dalton Panorama Tour are:

  • Outside John Dalton
  • John Dalton Entrance
  • John Dalton 2nd Floor
  • C2.04 PC Lab
  • C2.05 Mac Lab
  • C0.14 Lecture Theatre
  • John Dalton East Hallway
  • John Dalton East E0.34 Lecture Theatre
  • John Dalton East E1.45 Lecture Theatre
  • John Dalton East Student Hub
  • John Dalton East Window PC's

Mac Lab




The panorama below is showing the Mac Lab C2.05 located on the 2nd floor in the John Dalton Building. The number of students which this lab can hold is 36 students. Each lab session lasts for 2 hours. Students can access the iMac's which are available near to the door when a lab session is taking place for them to quietly enter and exit when a lab session is taking place otherwise students can drop in when there is no lab session taking place. Weekly Lab sessions are displayed on a timetable on the room door outside.

The software's which lectures teach students to use are:

  • Adobe Creative Cloud
  • Microsoft Office
  • Eclipse
  • Processing
  • Motion
  • Logisim
  • Android Studio
  • Mars
  • Text Wrangler
  • Final Cut Pro
